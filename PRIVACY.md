# Privacy & Data Deletion Policy

**CMU Insta · Last updated May 16, 2026**

---

## What we collect

CMU Insta collects the following data submitted voluntarily by users through Instagram Direct Messages: full name, academic major, hometown, Instagram user ID (IGSID), CMU Andrew ID (email), and one photo. This data is collected solely for the purpose of creating and publishing an Instagram post on the CMU Insta account.

## How we use it

Your data is used exclusively to verify your CMU enrollment, generate your acceptance post, and publish it to the CMU Insta Instagram account. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your Andrew ID is used only for identity verification and is not published or shared publicly.

## Data storage

Your data is stored on servers operated by ScottyLabs at Carnegie Mellon University. Photos are stored as files on our server. All other data is stored in a private database. We retain your data for no longer than one year after your post is published, after which it is permanently deleted.

## Instagram data

CMU Insta uses the Instagram Graph API to receive Direct Messages and publish posts. We access only the data necessary to operate this service: your IGSID and message content. We do not access your followers, following list, or any other Instagram account data. Our use of Instagram data complies with Meta's Platform Terms.

## Data deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time before or after your post is published. To request deletion:

1. Email `cmuinsta@scottylabs.org` with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
2. Include your Andrew ID and Instagram username
3. We will permanently delete all data associated with your account within 7 days
4. If your post has already been published, we will also remove it from Instagram

## Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, contact ScottyLabs at `cmuinsta@scottylabs.org`. This policy applies to data collected through the CMU Insta Instagram account and cmuinsta.scottylabs.org.
