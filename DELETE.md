# Data Deletion Request

**CMU Insta · ScottyLabs**

To request deletion of all data CMU Insta holds about you, follow these steps:

1. Email `cmuinsta@scottylabs.org` with the subject line **"Data Deletion Request"**
2. Include your **Andrew ID** and **Instagram username** in the email body
3. We will permanently delete all data associated with your account within **7 days**
4. If your post has already been published to the CMU Insta Instagram account, we will also remove it

## What gets deleted

- Your name, major, and hometown
- Your Andrew ID
- Your Instagram user ID (IGSID)
- Your submitted photo
- All internal records associated with your submission
